About Us

To address the growing need of engineering talent with skills in digital technology, TCS, in partnership with leading academicians across India, has designed a curriculum for 4 years undergraduate program on Computer Science titled â€œComputer Science and Business Systems (CSBS)."   • Innovation Ability
  • Strong in Ethics and Life Values
